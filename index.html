Green Chili Anchovy Medan

Medan Green Chili with Anchovy is a vibrant and flavorful Indonesian condiment that originates from the city of Medan in North Sumatra. This sambal is a delightful blend of fresh green chilies, shallots, garlic, tomatoes, and other spices, combined with crispy fried anchovies. The result is a savory, spicy, and slightly tangy relish that adds a kick to any meal.
